About Me


I am an associate researcher in artificial intelligence with a background in electrical and computer engineering. I am current working with the Complex Event Recognition group at NCSR “Demokritos” in Athens, Greece. My research involves stream reasoning, (probabilistic) logic programming and composite event recognition. Some additional research areas that I find interesting are: temporal logics, multi-agent systems and neuro-symbolic AI.
